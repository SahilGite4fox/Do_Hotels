Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines our approach to handling any personal data you provide to us or that we collect from you when you access our website.

Please note, this policy is applicable solely to the information collected through our website or during your interactions with us. It does not cover the privacy practices of external sites linked from our website or interactions with third parties.

By using our website, you agree, confirm and consent to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy. Should there be any changes to this policy, we will update the version posted on our website to keep you informed about the data we collect, how it is used, and under what circumstances it may be disclosed, and it shall be your sole responsibility to check updates of this policy on our website.

YOUR INFORMATION

We may collect the following types of information from you:

  • Your full name

  • Your address and postal code

  • Contact details (e.g., email address and/or phone numbers)

  • General personal or professional information (e.g., your interests, experience with our services or products, and contact preferences)

  • Professional details if you apply for a job with us

  • Technical information about your device, such as:

    • IP address

    • Browser type

    • Operating system

    • Access times

    • Referring website address

We will only collect data that personally identifies you if you provide it by filling out forms on our website.

HOW YOUR INFORMATION IS USED

One International Real Estate Development L.L.C and its affiliates (including subsidiaries, our parent company, and its subsidiaries) may use your information in the following ways:

  • To present the content on our site in the most effective manner for you and your device

  • To enable interactive features of our site

  • To provide products, services, and information you have requested

  • To improve and develop our products and services

  • For internal record-keeping

  • To respond to your queries

  • To contact you for market research or marketing purposes, with your consent

  • To evaluate your application for employment and conduct subsequent interviews

We will not use your information for commercial purposes without your prior written consent.

In certain situations, we may disclose your information to third parties as follows:

  • If we sell or acquire any business or assets, to prospective buyers or sellers, or if we are acquired by a third party, in which case your personal data may be transferred

  • To comply with legal obligations or enforce any agreements with you

  • To protect our rights, property, or the safety of our employees, customers, or others

  • When sharing information with recruitment agents if you apply for a job with us

  • When required to comply with legal obligations or protect our customers' or others' rights

MARKETING USE

Occasionally, we may send you information about promotions, offers, and other relevant products and services.

If you prefer not to receive such marketing communications, you can unsubscribe by:

  • Sending a request to Customer Services at [contact address]

  • Using the unsubscribe option available in each marketing communication

STORING YOUR INFORMATION

We may transfer your information to countries outside the UAE, provided those countries offer a level of protection that meets or exceeds UAE data protection standards. If the protection level is not equivalent, we will take appropriate measures to ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws.

Your data may also be processed by staff working outside the UAE who are involved in fulfilling contracts, processing payments, and providing support services. By providing your information, you consent to this transfer, storage, and processing.

We will retain your personal information only as long as necessary. If you withdraw your consent for us to use your information for marketing purposes, we will stop sending you such communications. However, we may contact you if legally required or to fulfil contractual obligations related to your purchase or service.

Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy is intended to inform you how One International Real Estate Development L.L.C uses a technology called “cookies” and web server logs, to assist you in making informed decisions when using our site.

WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile phone browser by websites that you visit. Cookies are widely used in order to make websites work, or to improve the user experience, as well as to provide certain information to the owners of the website. Our cookies may include information about your online preferences and allow us to tailor our websites to your interests, and often include an anonymous unique identifier.

CONSENT TO USE OF COOKIES ON THIS WEBSITE

By continuing to use our site, you consent to our use of the cookies described in this policy. If you do not consent to our use of cookies, then please read the next section “Blocking Use of Cookies”.

BLOCKING USE OF COOKIES

You have the opportunity to set your devices to accept all cookies, to notify you when a cookie is issued, or not to receive cookies at any time. The last of these options means that certain personalized services cannot then be provided to you, which may result in you not being able to take full advantage of all of the features of our websites.

If you do not wish to receive cookies generally, or to object to certain cookies, you might need to change your browser settings. However, each browser is different, so please check the “Help” menu of your preferred browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences. If you do not change your cookie preference, this is a confirmation and acknowledgement from your end to your acceptance of cookies.

If you choose to block use of cookies, some aspects of the site may not work properly and you may not be able to access all or part of our site, where by doing so you approve not having the complete utilization of our site.

HOW DO WE USE COOKIES?

Information supplied by cookies can help us to analyse your use of our websites and help us to provide you with a better online user experience to the extent possible. The information our cookies collect is not used to identify who you are, or to send you advertising by e-mail or post. Further, we do not use cookies to provide targeted and personalised advertising to children.

WHAT TYPE OF COOKIES DO WE USE FOR WHAT PURPOSES?TECHNICALLY REQUIRED COOKIES

Some of our cookies are strictly necessary to use our websites or certain functionalities within them. If you block these functional cookies, you cannot use our site. This includes the cookies to register whether the user has consented to cookies being used by our website or not.

SESSION COOKIES AND PREFERENCE COOKIES

We might use session cookies to uniquely identify your browsing session on our site, to facilitate the functioning of our websites, such as to enable the user to navigate through the different pages. These cookies expire at the end of the user session (e.g., when the user closes the browser window). We might also use cookies that persist for a longer period in order to remember your preferences and choices on our websites.

Generally, those cookies will not have a life span exceeding two years.

ANALYTICS COOKIES

We might use analytics cookies in conjunction with our web server’s log files to enhance the performance of our websites by collecting aggregated information about how many visitors we have, where visitors have come to the site from and how they use the website (e.g. which pages most visitors go to most often).

We do not generally store any personal information that you provide to us in a cookie.

SOCIAL NETWORK COOKIES

We might integrate in our website features from third party websites, in particular from certain social networks. Those features sometimes come with scripts or other elements that can read, and sometimes place, cookies from those social networks on your device.

These cookies might allow the personalisation of advertising, to make advertising more relevant to your interests. We have no access to or control over those cookies or the data collected by those cookies, because we are only the operator of the One International Real Estate Development L.L.C website, however we would like to inform you and obtain consent for those elements, that is granted through your suage to our website.
